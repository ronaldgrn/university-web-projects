How to Play

This game was inspired by the card game Concentration. You simply turn over a pair of cards. If they are the same, awesome. If not, turn them facedown again.
The objective of the game is to get all the cards turned faceup using the above method, before the timer runs out (3 minutes)
The cards used here are tarot cards, inspried by the popular "Tarot of Marseilles" deck.
