What we do

To achieve our goal of alleviating poverty in the Caribbean. P&Y Green has dedicated itself to providing the following services

  • We extend low-interest financial assistance in the form of loans for development projects and programs

  • We offer grants in support of technical assistance, research and emergency relief

  • We facilitate and host training workshops, seminars and discussions on development and economic issues affecting the region
